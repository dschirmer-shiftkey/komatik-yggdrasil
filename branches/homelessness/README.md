# Branch: Homelessness

> Data-driven approaches to understanding and reducing homelessness in specific
> urban areas.

## Problem Space

Homelessness is a complex, multi-causal crisis driven by housing affordability,
mental health, substance use, economic dislocation, and systemic policy failures.
Solutions are deeply local — what works in one city may not transfer directly to
another due to differences in climate, housing markets, social services, legal
frameworks, and cultural context.

Yggdrasil's approach is to fund city/metro-level seedlings that research their
local problem in depth, while sharing structural insights (intervention
taxonomies, data methodologies, policy pattern analysis) upward to the branch
level for cross-city learning.

## Scope Floor

**Metro-level** — seedlings on this branch are scoped to metropolitan areas or
cities. Contributors wanting to focus on a specific neighborhood within an
existing metro seedling should contribute to the metro-level seedling rather
than creating a new one.
