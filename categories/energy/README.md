# Category: Energy

> Distributed renewable generation and open hardware for communities without
> reliable electricity.

## Problem Space

Over 750 million people lack access to reliable electricity. Centralized grid
extension is prohibitively expensive for dispersed rural populations. The
solution must be distributed, affordable, maintainable with local skills, and
built from components available in-country.

## Scope Floor

**Region-level** — seeds in this category are scoped to sub-continental
regions (e.g., "Sub-Saharan Africa", "Southeast Asia"). Contributors wanting
to focus on a specific country within an existing region should contribute to
the regional seed rather than creating a new one.
