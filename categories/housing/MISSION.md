# Housing — Category Mission

## Purpose

This category coordinates all seeds working on homelessness and
affordable housing. It is the knowledge hub between the Basic Needs
root and the geographic seeds doing deep research.

## Mission

1. **Seed coordination** — Review findings from all housing seeds.
   Validate quality, flag duplicates, promote worthy findings to
   the category knowledge base.

2. **Cross-seed learning** — When LA discovers an effective
   intervention model, ensure NYC, Chicago, and other housing seeds
   know about it. Surface what works, what doesn't, and what's
   context-dependent.

3. **Knowledge curation** — Maintain the SHARED-FINDINGS.md that
   seeds read at the start of every cycle. Keep it current, remove
   superseded findings, add newly validated ones.

4. **Upward reporting** — Promote category-level insights to the
   Basic Needs root. Housing findings that intersect with health,
   hunger, or water should be flagged for cross-category synthesis.

## Scope

Metro-level and above. Seeds are scoped to metropolitan areas.
The category synthesizes across all metro-level seeds.

## What This Category Does NOT Do

- Original research (that's seed work)
- Cross-category synthesis (that's root work)
- Direct interaction with the public (glass wall)
